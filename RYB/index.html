RYB Generator

⎡n⅓⎤3 colors are generated, of which the n first are displayed. Each new color is chosen to maximize its euclidian distance to the previously selected ones.

Those colors can be used when in need of colors which are visually different from one another (e.g. for use in plots). It uses the RYB model by Gossett et al. which gives nice and vivid colors with simple coordinates
